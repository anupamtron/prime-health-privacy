# Privacy Policy for Prime Health

**Last updated:** July 9, 2026

Prime Health ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

Prime Health may collect the following information:

- Weight and health information entered by you.
- Food logs and activity records.
- App usage information for improving the app.

## Advertising

Prime Health may display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information such as device identifiers and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads according to Google's policies.

Learn more:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## In-App Purchases

Prime Health may offer optional premium features through Google Play Billing. Payment information is securely handled by Google. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

Prime Health is not intended for children under the age of 13.
